How to Play:

- Each player starts at spot 1.

- Take turns to roll the dice. To roll the dice, click on the dice. Your counter will automatically move forward the number of spaces you rolled.

- To move to next player click on the "move to next player" button.

- If you land at the bottom of a ladder, you get to move up to the top of the ladder

- If you land on the head of a snake, you slide down to the bottom of the snake.

- First player to get to space 100 is the winner
